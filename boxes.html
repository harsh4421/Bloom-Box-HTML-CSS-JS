KEEPING THEM FRESH

Flower Care Guide

Follow these simple steps to keep your blooms beautiful for longer.

Universal First-Day Care

  1. Unpack immediately and remove any packaging from stems.
  2. Fill a clean vase with fresh, cool (not cold) water.
  3. Cut 1—2 cm off the bottom of each stem at a 45° angle.
  4. Add the included flower food packet to the water.
  5. Remove any leaves that fall below the waterline.
  6. Place away from direct sunlight, heat vents, and ripe fruit.
